Kühne Logistics Day


        The idea of this event is to create a platform to network for all logistics experts and to strengthen the exchange of ideas and to further promote the education in the field of logistics.
        Kühne Logistics Day is held once every year in Germany and once every two years in Shanghai. Since more than 16 years the Kühne Foundation organizes annually the “Logistics Day of Kühne Foundation” at different universities at which the Foundation holds a chair for logistics management.
